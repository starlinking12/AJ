# J.A.R.V.I.S.

Just A Rather Very Intelligent System

A production-grade, voice-first AI assistant with arc reactor UI.

---

## What It Does

- Voice in, voice out. No typing. No chat.
- Breathing arc reactor circle on a black desktop.
- "Wake up" to activate. "Sleep" to rest.
- Controls your computer. Opens apps, shows maps, plays music.
- Sees your screen and camera only when asked.
- Remembers everything. Local memory. No cloud required.
